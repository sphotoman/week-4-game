Crystal Collector!

You are playing a game in which a random number is genereated and displayed below.

Below the random number are 4 crystal that at the start of each game a number (value) random assigned to them.

Your task it to click on the crystals, determine their value and then continue to click on the appropriate crystals until your total score is equal to the random number. If they match, you win. If not, you lose.

Each time you win or lose, the game restets itself. The random number is reset and the values of the Crystals change and you can play again.
